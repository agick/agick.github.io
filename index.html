Lets dig at bit deeper...

Looking at everything that has been said during the meetings at the parliment, we want to find out more about how they use language.

We will start by looking at the sentiment of the parties.

The sentiment score is a relative number where we have taken into account the total amount of text for each party. This allows to better compare the parties

Looking at the data we can see that the parties with the most extreme political views (both left and right leaning), also ranks lowest in sentiment score. Coincident? We don't think so.

We will now look into the lix number, which is a number indicating readability

The lix analysis shows that all parties use language with medium readability. We can also see that the two parties ranking lowest are parties based in greenland, which might explain the lower score.
